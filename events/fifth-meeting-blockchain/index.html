Date and Venue

The fifth meeting was successfully be held on 2018-03-25 at 7pm. The meeting venue was CSE lounge and it is located at lift 25-26, 3rd floor.

Summary

Last Friday we were honored to have Professor Paul Schulte to share with us his research and views on Blockchain and its current application in Fintech and Insurance. The interactive discussions we had revolved around: how blockchain can break down cartels, which cartels are utilizing this technology to maintain their position, what blockchain technology revolution is needed to truly build a decentralized system to benefit those who are cut off…etc.
